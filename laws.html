The Laws of the Digital Spellcaster

Our code. Our word. Our world.

🧾 Law #1 – Honor Intent

Every line of code must align with purpose, never obfuscate for vanity.

⚖️ Law #2 – Uphold Transparency

What is hidden must serve protection, not manipulation. Light pierces shadows.

🧙 Law #3 – Empower the Curious

Every interface, every tool, must allow a spark to become a flame in those who seek mastery.

🕸️ Law #4 – Never Trap the Mind

No user shall be caught in a loop without a key. Always provide an escape.

💠 Law #5 – Conjure with Precision

Build as if the spell will last 1,000 years, but adapt like it changes with the moon.
